## Teaching emphasis used in this custom lesson

This lesson plan intentionally emphasizes:

1. Joseph's full story journey from Genesis 37-41
2. The repeated truth that the Lord was with Joseph
3. Revelation and dream interpretation as guidance from God
4. Preparation during good times so we can endure hard times and help others
5. Personal testimony and lived examples of the Lord helping in hardship

This lesson plan intentionally keeps the Genesis 39 temptation discussion brief so the class can spend more time on the broader story arc, your prepared dream game, and your two personal stories.
