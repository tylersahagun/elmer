# Tldraw board outline

Board title: Joseph's Journey - Genesis 37-41

## Purpose

Create a bright, easy-to-follow visual for a 12-year-old Sunday School class that:

- shows the story of Joseph in sequence
- labels the main scripture references
- highlights repeating themes and gospel learnings
- points back to official Church study resources

## Main sections on the board

### 1. Title banner

- Joseph's Journey
- Genesis 37-41
- Main truth: The Lord was with Joseph

### 2. Story journey timeline

Use a left-to-right path with arrows between stages:

1. Favorite son, coat, and dreams  
   Genesis 37:3-11

2. Betrayed, thrown in a pit, and sold  
   Genesis 37:18-28

3. Potiphar's house, faithful choices, prison  
   Genesis 39:1-6, 20-23

4. Dreams in prison  
   Genesis 40:5-8

5. Pharaoh's dreams and Joseph's counsel  
   Genesis 41:14-36

6. Joseph leads Egypt and prepares for famine  
   Genesis 41:38-57

### 3. Repeating themes

- The Lord was with Joseph
- God can guide through revelation
- Hard things can prepare us for future service
- Faithfulness matters even when life feels unfair

### 4. Learnings for the class

- God does not forget us in hard times
- We can keep choosing the right
- Preparation now helps us bless people later
- We can give God credit for our gifts and answers

### 5. Class connection callout

- Guess the dream game goes near the prison and Pharaoh dream section
- Personal story callout goes near the hardship theme section

### 6. Official source box

List these references:

- Come, Follow Me - For Home and Church: Old Testament 2026, lesson 11
- Games, Stories, Activities - Genesis 37-41
- Old Testament Seminary Manual 2026 - Genesis 37-41
- Scripture Helps: Old Testament - Genesis 37-41

## Tone and visual style

- bright but readable colors
- large text
- simple summaries instead of crowded paragraphs
- clear arrows so the class can follow the story quickly
- use a hopeful tone, not a dark or overly heavy one
