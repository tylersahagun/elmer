## Story journey lane

### 1. Beloved son and inspired dreams

- Scripture: [Genesis 37:3-11](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/37?lang=eng&id=p3-p11#p3)
- Key idea: Joseph receives a special coat and shares inspired dreams.
- Teaching note: God can reveal truth.

### 2. Pit and slavery

- Scripture: [Genesis 37:18-28](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/37?lang=eng&id=p18-p28#p18), [Genesis 37:36](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/37?lang=eng&id=p36#p36)
- Key idea: Joseph's brothers betray him and sell him into Egypt.
- Teaching note: Other people's choices can hurt us, but God still sees us.

### 3. The Lord blesses Joseph in Egypt

- Scripture: [Genesis 39:1-6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/39?lang=eng&id=p1-p6#p1)
- Key idea: Joseph serves faithfully in Potiphar's house and the Lord prospers him.
- Teaching note: God helps us in unfair places.

### 4. Prison, but not alone

- Scripture: [Genesis 39:20-23](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/39?lang=eng&id=p20-p23#p20)
- Key idea: Joseph is falsely accused and imprisoned, but the Lord is still with him.
- Teaching note: Hard times do not mean God left.

### 5. Dreams in prison

- Scripture: [Genesis 40:5-23](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/40?lang=eng&id=p5-p23#p5)
- Key idea: Joseph interprets the butler's and baker's dreams and gives God the credit.
- Teaching note: Revelation points us to God.

### 6. Pharaoh's dreams and wise preparation

- Scripture: [Genesis 41:14-43](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/41?lang=eng&id=p14-p43#p14), [Genesis 41:53-57](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/41?lang=eng&id=p53-p57#p53)
- Key idea: Joseph interprets Pharaoh's dreams and prepares Egypt for famine.
- Teaching note: Preparation blesses many people.

## Big teachings lane

### Trust

- Theme: The Lord is with us in hardship.
- Scriptures: [Genesis 39:2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/39?lang=eng&id=p2#p2), [Genesis 39:21](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/39?lang=eng&id=p21#p21), [Genesis 39:23](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/39?lang=eng&id=p23#p23)
- Teaching move: share the pre-mission story and the mission president story here.

### Listen

- Theme: God gives revelation.
- Scriptures: [Genesis 37:5-11](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/37?lang=eng&id=p5-p11#p5); [Genesis 40:8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/40?lang=eng&id=p8#p8); [Genesis 41:16](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/41?lang=eng&id=p16#p16), [Genesis 41:25](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/41?lang=eng&id=p25#p25), [Genesis 41:38](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/41?lang=eng&id=p38#p38)
- Teaching move: use the prepared "guess the dreams" game here.

### Prepare

- Theme: Preparation brings peace.
- Scriptures: [Genesis 41:33-36](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/41?lang=eng&id=p33-p36#p33), [Genesis 41:53-57](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/41?lang=eng&id=p53-p57#p53); [Doctrine and Covenants 38:30](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/38?lang=eng&id=p30#p30)
- Teaching move: invite youth to name ways they can prepare spiritually now.
