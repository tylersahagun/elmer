**Heading 3:** The Signal Ontology (v1)

**Table (Signal | Why CRMs Miss It):**
| Signal | Why CRMs Miss It |
|--------|------------------|
| Deal Stage Confidence | Reps lag on updates; conversations reveal reality |
| Decision Maker Engagement | CRM tracks contacts, not influence |
| Competitive Mentions | CRM has a field; conversations reveal nuance |
| Objection Themes | CRM captures notes, not patterns |
| Commitment Tracking | CRM has no concept of verbal commitments |
| Champion Strength | CRM tracks contacts, not advocacy |
| Next Steps Adherence | CRM captures next steps; no follow-through |
| Buying Signal Density | CRM has no density concept |
| Stakeholder Sentiment | CRM is purely factual |
| Deal Velocity | CRM tracks dates, not momentum |
