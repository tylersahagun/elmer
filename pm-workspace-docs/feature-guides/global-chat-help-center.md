# Global Chat: Help Center Guide

**Feature status:** Early access  
**Availability:** Workspaces with Global Chat enabled

---

## What is Global Chat?

Global Chat is AskElephant's AI-first chat experience that stays available across the app to answer questions about your meetings, customers, and workflows. It can use the context of the page you are on (company, meeting, or contact) to give more specific answers.

---

## Who it's for

- Sales reps preparing for calls or recalling commitments
- Sales leaders coaching across multiple reps and accounts
- CSMs getting context before renewals or escalations
- RevOps reviewing capture coverage and patterns

---

## Where to find it

### Web

- Open **Chat** from the top navigation, or use the keyboard shortcut (Cmd/Ctrl + K in the new experience, Cmd/Ctrl + L in legacy).
- Chat may appear as a right-side panel or an overlay depending on your workspace layout.

### Mobile

- Open **Global Chat** from the home screen prompt.
- Ask your question in plain English.

---

## How it works

1. Open Global Chat from anywhere.
2. Ask a question in natural language.
3. Global Chat uses the page context when available.
4. If Internal Search is enabled, it can look across your meeting history.
5. Answers stream in. You can ask follow-up questions or start a new chat.

---

## What data it uses

- Meeting recordings, transcripts, summaries, and notes available to you.
- Page context from the company, meeting, or contact you are viewing.
- Results always respect workspace permissions.

---

## Using Internal Search with Global Chat

If your workspace has Internal Search enabled:

1. Open Global Chat.
2. Click **Tools** (puzzle icon).
3. Toggle **Internal search** on.
4. Ask your question and review the results panel.

---

## Example questions

**Sales reps**

- "What did we discuss with Acme in the last 30 days?"
- "Show me objections from my last call with Sarah Johnson."

**Sales leaders**

- "Find discovery calls where pricing came up last week."
- "Show me meetings with enterprise accounts this month."

**CSMs**

- "What risks did DataStream mention this quarter?"
- "Show me all meetings tied to NovaCorp before renewal."

**RevOps**

- "Which meetings from this week do not have summaries?"
- "Who has not recorded a meeting in the last 14 days?"

---

## Tips for better results

- **Open from the most relevant page** (company, meeting, or contact).
- **Add time windows** like "last 2 weeks" or "in December".
- **Name the account or contact** when you want a specific result.
- **Start broad, then narrow** with a follow-up question.

---

## Common questions

**Is Global Chat the same as Internal Search?**  
Global Chat is the interface. Internal Search is an optional tool that helps it search across your meeting history.

**Why are answers better on some pages?**  
Global Chat uses the page context to make answers more specific.

**Does it work on mobile?**  
Yes, when Mobile Global Chat is enabled for your workspace.

---

## Troubleshooting

**I do not see the Chat button**  
Try Cmd/Ctrl + K (or Cmd/Ctrl + L in legacy). If chat still does not appear, your workspace may not have Global Chat enabled yet. Contact your AskElephant admin or support.

**The shortcut does not open chat**  
Some workspaces use Cmd/Ctrl + K (new experience) while others use Cmd/Ctrl + L (legacy).

**Chat closes when I navigate**  
Some workspaces use a layout that keeps chat open across navigation; others open it as an overlay that closes when you move to a new page.

**Answers feel too generic**  
Open chat from the most relevant page and add a time window. If available, enable Internal Search.

---

## Known limitations

- Availability depends on workspace feature access.
- Answers only use recorded meetings and available workspace data.
- Results respect workspace permissions.

---

## Need help?

If Global Chat is not showing up or results look off, reach out to your AskElephant contact or support.
