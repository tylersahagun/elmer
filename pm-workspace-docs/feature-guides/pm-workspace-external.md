# PM Workspace Guide (External Version)

This is a public-facing version of the PM Workspace overview. It removes
internal tooling, Slack identifiers, and confidential workflows.

---

## What This Workspace Is

An outcome-driven product operations system that connects research, planning,
prototyping, validation, and reporting into a repeatable workflow.

---

## Core Principles

- Outcomes over outputs
- Human-centered AI (orchestration, not replacement)
- Trust and privacy first
- Consistency before speed

---

## What It Enables

- Turn research into requirements and prototypes quickly
- Keep artifacts organized by initiative phase
- Run repeatable reporting and planning workflows
- Teach a consistent product operating model

---

## High-Level Workflow Playbooks

### 1. Research → PRD

Use structured research to define the problem, then create a requirements
document with clear outcome chains.

### 2. PRD → Prototype → Validation

Prototype rapidly and validate with repeatable evaluation steps.

### 3. Status → Reporting

Keep stakeholder updates current with lightweight, repeatable reporting.

### 4. Signals → Synthesis → Hypothesis

Detect themes across signals and convert them into testable hypotheses.

---

## Teaching Others

Run a short walkthrough with:

1. A system overview (why, how, where artifacts live)
2. A single workflow demo
3. A short exercise and Q&A

---

## Note on Internal Tools

Internal workflows integrate with team tools (task tracking, communication,
analytics). Those integrations are intentionally omitted from this version.
