# Hypothesis: Chief Of Staff Daily Hub

## Status

- **Current:** active
- **Created:** 2026-01-29
- **Last Updated:** 2026-01-29
- **Linked Initiative:** chief-of-staff-hub

---

## Problem Statement

**Who:** Sales reps, sales leaders, RevOps

**What:** There is no clear daily entry point to review what automation did, what needs approval, and what is scheduled.

**When:** At the start of the day and after meetings when users need to act quickly.

**Impact:** Users must dig through meetings and workflows, which creates cognitive overload and reduces daily adoption.

> One-sentence summary: As a sales leader or rep, I struggle to understand what to do next because there is no daily hub that summarizes actions and approvals, which causes lower engagement and slower time-to-value.

