## Problem Statement

**Who:** Product leadership and stakeholders (Sam, execs, design/eng reviewers)

**What:** There is no shared, concrete set of meeting‑recap artifacts to align on, which makes direction feel abstract and slows consensus.

**When:** During board/pitch updates and prototype reviews where visuals are required.

**Impact:** Decision‑making and prioritization stall; the Chief‑of‑Staff direction feels conceptual rather than grounded.

> One-sentence summary: As a stakeholder, I struggle to align on the meeting recap direction because we lack concrete artifact visuals, which delays decisions and slows momentum.
