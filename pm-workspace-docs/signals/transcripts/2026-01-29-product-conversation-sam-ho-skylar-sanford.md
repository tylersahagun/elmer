## TL;DR

Sam and Skylar outlined a board-ready vision for AskElephant as the “revenue operating system” (cursor-for-GTM) powered by agentic learning with human feedback. The conversation converged on a chief‑of‑staff led interface (daily brief, meeting prep, post‑call admin) with semi‑autonomous agents that learn and coordinate across teams, and a roadmap that sequences standard agents first, then seamless integration, then platform expansion.

## Key Decisions

- Frame the product as a **revenue operating system** (cursor for GTM teams), not a point solution.
- Position **agentic learning with human feedback (ALHF)** as the key differentiator that compounds value over time.
- Use **semi‑autonomous orchestration** (human conductor + lead agents + sub‑agents) rather than “fully autonomous.”
- Sequence delivery as **daily brief → meeting prep → post‑call admin**, then deeper workflows.
- Meet users where they already work (Slack/voice), then bridge into the platform.

## Feature Requests

- **ALHF feedback controls**: thumbs up/down → lightweight voice feedback for “why.”
- **Personalization layers**: base prompt + business‑specific + individual preferences.
- **Chief of staff daily brief**: upcoming meetings, yesterday review, decisions.
- **3‑bucket daily review**: auto‑executed, approval‑needed, decision‑needed.
- **Proactive coaching agent**: weekly coaching review, not per‑meeting.
- **Slack/voice interface**: meet users where they work; “Huddl with agent.”
- **Agent marketplace**: curated skills library (SkillsMP‑style).

## Strategic Alignment

- ✅ Strong alignment with “revenue outcome system” vision and AI‑first UX.
- ✅ Reinforces trust + orchestration (humans conduct; agents execute).
- ⚠️ Needs explicit outcome metrics for “chief of staff” adoption/retention.
