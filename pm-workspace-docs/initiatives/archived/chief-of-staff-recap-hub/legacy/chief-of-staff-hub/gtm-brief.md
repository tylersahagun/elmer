# GTM Brief: Chief Of Staff Hub

## Overview

Position the Chief Of Staff Hub as the proactive daily command center that turns AskElephant into the revenue operating system. The GTM focus is a pilot rollout with clear trust messaging and persona-specific narratives.

**Related PRD:** [prd.md](./prd.md)  
**Status:** Draft  
**Launch Tier:** P2 (pilot + internal enablement)

---

## Value Proposition

- **Sales Leaders:** Review approvals and coach the team without digging through workflows.
- **Sales Reps:** Start the day with a clear list of what is done, what needs approval, and what is next.
- **RevOps:** Governance and auditability for automated actions.
- **CSMs:** Proactive risk surface and scheduled follow-ups.

## Key Messages

1. **"Your day in one place"** — done, needs approval, scheduled.
2. **"Trust without the tax"** — approvals only when needed.
3. **"Proactive, not reactive"** — auto-run low-risk actions with clear visibility.

---

## Competitive Positioning

- **Generic tools:** show data, not decisions.
- **AskElephant:** orchestrates actions with human approval, tied to revenue outcomes.
