# Prototype Notes: Chief of Staff Experience

## Experience frame

Chief of Staff is a proactive workspace that shows:

1. What happened
2. What changed
3. What needs approval
4. What to do next

## Parent IA

- Chief of Staff Home
- Meeting Summary
- Meeting Prep
- Daily Brief
- Weekly Brief
- Action Items

## Interaction principles

- Artifact-first, not workflow-first
- Action-first ordering
- AI-assisted edits in context
- Confidence and evidence visible where automation is involved
- Historical continuity for day/week experiences

## Sub-initiative prototype targets

### Meeting Summary

- Section templates by meeting type
- AI rewrite and edit suggestions
- "Use this template going forward" control

### Meeting Prep

- Pre-call packet with prior meeting highlights, CRM changes, risks, and proposed agenda

### Daily Brief

- "Newspaper" style morning/evening digest
- Snapshot mode for historical navigation
- Action cards with direct execution

### Weekly Brief

- Goal-progress rollup and trend blocks
- Carry-forward commitments and stale items

### Action Items

- Unified queue
- Evidence-backed recommendations
- One-click schedule follow-up using calendar availability

## Open prototype questions

- Should daily brief update in real time or append timestamped deltas?
- Which interaction pattern best supports "approve all low-risk" behavior?
- How much customization should be available before introducing complexity?
