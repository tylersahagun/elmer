# Decisions: Chief of Staff Experience

## 2026-02-17 - Parent initiative split into 5 sub-initiatives

- **Decision:** Establish one parent Chief of Staff initiative with five sub-initiatives:
  - Meeting Summary
  - Meeting Prep
  - Daily Brief
  - Weekly Brief
  - Action Items
- **Why:** Preserves one strategic narrative while allowing scoped execution, ownership, and measurement by outcome.
- **Participants:** Tyler (PM), synthesized from Sam and Rob feedback.

## 2026-02-17 - Meeting Summary promoted to first-class artifact

- **Decision:** Remove meeting summary from workflow-first interpretation and treat it as a primary artifact with template + AI edit loop.
- **Why:** Users experience current flow as configuration overhead and buried outputs.
- **Expected impact:** Faster time-to-value, higher recap consumption, and stronger trust.

## 2026-02-17 - Daily and Weekly Brief must be cross-signal

- **Decision:** Daily/weekly briefs will include meetings plus operational context (CRM, Slack/comms, and calendar/task signals).
- **Why:** Business execution does not run on meetings alone; meeting-only views miss critical context.
- **Expected impact:** Better prioritization and stronger action quality.

## 2026-02-17 - Action-first hierarchy

- **Decision:** Chief of Staff screens and artifacts lead with actions and approvals before detailed insight blocks.
- **Why:** Action orientation improves execution behavior and perceived product value.
- **Expected impact:** Improved action completion and recurring engagement.

## 2026-02-17 - Historical continuity required

- **Decision:** Daily and weekly outputs must support historical snapshots and carry-forward logic.
- **Why:** Users need continuity and confidence in what changed over time.
- **Open item:** Determine immutable snapshot policy in v1 or v1.1.
