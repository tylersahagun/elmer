# Competitive Landscape: Meeting Prep

**Initiative:** Chief of Staff — Meeting Prep
**Date:** 2026-02-17
**Analysis scope:** Revenue intelligence, meeting AI, CRM AI hubs, automation builders
**Owner:** Tyler

---

## TL;DR

AskElephant Meeting Prep competes in a crowded space where Gong, Clari, and Chorus deliver **post-meeting briefs** and **deal-level intelligence**. Pre-call prep specifically is under-served: most competitors focus on post-call summaries. Gong AI Briefer and HubSpot Breeze offer the closest analogs. The gap: **persona-aware, meeting-triggered prep packets** that leverage prior recaps and open actions—delivered in the right window (e.g., 60 min pre-meeting) with confidence in "what changed since last conversation."

**Primary differentiation:**

1. **Prep as input from recap output** — Prior meeting takeaways and open risks flow into prep; not siloed
2. **Persona-aware prep blocks** — Sellers vs CSM vs leader get relevant focus
3. **Time-windowed delivery** — Right moment before the call, not buried in a dashboard
4. **Confidence in recency** — Clear "what changed since last touch"

## Patterns to Adopt

| Pattern                                        | Source         | Rationale                                            |
| ---------------------------------------------- | -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Multi-level briefs (contact/call/deal/account) | Gong           | Users expect context at different grains             |
| Morning digest with actions                    | Clari Groove   | Drives engagement; complement with meeting-triggered |
| Prep from CRM                                  | HubSpot Breeze | Baseline for HubSpot users                           |
| Post-meeting briefs to inbox                   | Chorus         | Delivery pattern; apply to pre-call                  |

---

## Patterns to Reject

| Pattern                          | Source      | Rationale                                |
| -------------------------------- | ----------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Insight-heavy dashboards as prep | Gong, Clari | Prep should be artifact, not exploration |
| Generic "check your calendar"    | Many        | Needs meeting-specific context           |
| No linkage to prior recap        | Most        | Siloed prep loses continuity             |
