# Deprecate Deprecating the Pipe Dream - GTM Brief

## Positioning

Reliable, trusted integrations without vendor fragility. Composio replaces Pipedream so workflows and chat actions remain dependable with clear auth attribution.

## Target Audience

- Primary: RevOps (owners of integrations and automation)
- Secondary: Sales Leaders and Sales Reps (workflow adopters)

## Value Proposition

- Fewer automation failures.
- Clear attribution for integration actions.
- Reduced vendor risk with a modern integration backbone.

## Launch Approach

- Internal release first (feature flag).
- Controlled rollout to pilot workspaces once parity and auth scope are confirmed.

## Enablement Needs

- Internal FAQ explaining auth scope (workspace vs user).
- Support playbook for migration issues and parity gaps.

## Risks & Mitigations

- **Risk:** Missing integration parity → **Mitigation:** Communicate coverage and provide fallback plans.
- **Risk:** Auth confusion → **Mitigation:** Scope UI + attribution messaging.

## Success Signals

- Increase in integration connection success rate.
- Decrease in integration-related support tickets.
