**Theme:** Tyler fills every silent moment (audiobooks, work, videos) to avoid feeling/processing stress. Not intentional avoidance - but automatic response to keep moving.

#### Jamis's Personal System for Managing Overwhelm

##### Priority Hierarchy (Morning and Night)
**Jamis's morning priorities (in order):**
1. Sleep (7 hours non-negotiable)
2. Workout (30 minutes of something)
3. Unwind (10-20 min YouTube golf/DP show before work)
4. Work

**Jamis's night priorities (in order):**
1. Sleep (7 hours)
2. Emma (relationship time)
3. Unwind (any kind of mental break)
4. Work (only if 1-3 are secured)

**Quote from Jamis:** "I will do work at night, especially with this partner thing and everything that I've taken on if I know I'm going to get 7 hours of sleep, if I know that I'm going to spend any amount of time with Emma, if I know I'm going to be able to watch YouTube golf or a basketball game or scroll like any kind of unwinding read, then I will do work... **Theme:** Explicit hierarchy prevents work from consuming everything. If priorities 1-3 aren't met, work doesn't happen - protects against burnout spiral.

**Jamis quote:** "Every single night, I physically write the 5 things that stress me out the most and it just turns the water on, but then it allows my brain to point at something the next day.
