# Competitive Profile: Gong (gong.io)

**Initiative:** Agent Command Center  
**Date:** 2026-02-16  
**Competitor:** Gong — Revenue AI OS  
**Relevance:** Meeting intelligence + CRM automation + daily proactive hub (direct competitor)

---

## TL;DR

**Gong** is the dominant Revenue Intelligence platform, positioned in 2025 as the **"Revenue AI OS"** (Revenue AI Operating System) — the #1 AI operating system for revenue teams. They target CROs, sales leaders, RevOps, and CSMs with a unified platform that captures conversations, generates AI insights, and orchestrates revenue workflows. Their daily rep experience centers on a **homepage with Account AI** (ask questions about deals/accounts), **snapshot widgets** (forecast, pipeline, to-dos, conversations), and a **sidebar** linking Engage, Conversations, Insights, Deals, Coaching, and Activity. Recent launches (Oct 2025) include **Gong Orchestrate** for GTM strategy execution, **AI Data Extractor** for zero-touch CRM population, and a suite of **specialized AI agents**. Gong is enterprise-heavy (5,000+ customers, $100–200/user/mo typical), with strong G2/Capterra ratings but complaints about cost, Smart Tracker maintenance, and implementation complexity.

## 3. Key Features (Agent Command Center–Relevant)

### 3.1 Daily Hub / Homepage Experience

**What a rep sees when they log in:**

- **Top toolbar**: Search (account, contact, call), workspace switcher, settings, help, community, Academy, The Edge (revenue insights), What's new
- **Sidebar**: Homepage, Engage, Conversations, Insights, Deals, Coaching, Activity — role-based, expandable sub-categories
- **Homepage (center)**:
  - **Account AI**: Natural-language questions about accounts and deals; "Get briefed" for structured briefs on specific topics
  - **Collaboration panel**: Received (comments, mentions, shared calls, scores, feedback requests) and Sent
  - **Your forecast**: Target, gap, coverage, shortcut to update
  - **Your pipeline**: Total open deal value, portion at risk
  - **What's on your plate for today**: Engage to-dos to complete
  - **Conversations**: Live + recently recorded calls; tabs: Your calls, Team, Company, Your streams, Recently played, Listen later
  - **Prepare for meetings**: Scheduled meetings + AI meeting prep
  - **Announcements**, **Gong essentials**, **Pro tips**

**UX pattern:** Snapshot-first, question-driven (Account AI), with shortcuts to core work areas. Role and company setup determine what appears.

**Sources:** [Intro to the homepage](https://help.gong.io/docs/intro-to-the-homepage), [Explore your Gong workspace](https://help.gong.io/docs/explore-your-gong-workspace)

### 3.2 Deal Execution / Deal Workspace

- **Deal Board**: Pipeline view from bird's-eye down to email-level; AI risk flagging, deal warnings, corrective action recommendations
- **Deal Execution**: Reality-based forecasting, AI-backed pipeline inspection, deal health vs. at-risk
- **Customization**: Filter by CRM fields, tabs by stage/forecast category, multiple boards, share/duplicate/edit
- **Claimed outcomes**: 29% shorter sales cycles, 31% of at-risk deals saved, 50% win-rate increase (Gong marketing)

**Sources:** [Deal Board](https://www.gong.io/deal-board), [Deal Execution](https://www.gong.io/deal-execution), [Pipeline review](https://help.gong.io/docs/pipeline-review-use-deal-boards)

### 3.3 AI-Generated Insights and Recommendations

- **AI Tasker**: Surfaces high-impact tasks; AI ranks and schedules activities by customer data, deal urgency, revenue potential
- **AI Ask Anything**: Natural-language questions about calls, deals, accounts, contacts; instant answers across customer lifecycle
- **AI Deal Reviewer / Monitor / Predictor**: Qualify pipeline, detect risk, predict close likelihood
- **AI Revenue Predictor**: Forecast revenue from real customer conversations (300+ signals, 20% more accurate than CRM-only)
- **AI Theme Spotter**: Recurring themes (pain points, goals, competitors, objections)

## 4. Pricing

Gong does **not** publish standard public pricing. Structure:

| Component                 | Range                                                                                          |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Platform fee (annual)** | Small (10–25 users): $5K–$15K/yr; Mid (25–100): $15K–$35K/yr; Enterprise (100+): $35K–$75K+/yr |
| **Per-seat (monthly)**    | Full: $100–$175; Manager: $125–$200; Listen-only: $40–$75                                      |
| **Typical total**         | $100–$200/user/mo + platform fee; 15–30% negotiated discounts common                           |

**Plans:** Gong Core Professional, Gong Forecast, Gong Engage (modular add-ons: Enable Essentials, Forecast Essentials, Data Cloud).

**Real-world:** 10-user team ≈ $18K–$35K/yr; 50-user ≈ $75K–$150K+/yr. Implementation 3–6 months, $50K–$150K with vendors; true TCO with Clari stacking can reach $410–550/user/mo.

**Sources:** [Gong pricing](https://www.gong.io/pricing/), [Plans and seats](https://help.gong.io/docs/plans-and-seats), [CloudTalk Gong pricing](https://www.cloudtalk.io/blog/gong-pricing/), [Oliv.ai Gong reviews](https://www.oliv.ai/blog/gong-reviews)

## 5. Key Strengths (G2 / Capterra / Gartner)

- **Centralized intelligence**: All account data in one place, multiple views

**Sources:** [G2 Gong](https://www.g2.com/compare/gong-vs-hubspot-sales-hub), [Gartner Gong](https://www.gartner.com/reviews/market/revenue-intelligence/vendor/gong/product/gong), [Capterra Gong](https://www.capterra.ca/reviews/157969/gong-io)

## 6. Key Weaknesses (G2 / Capterra / Peerspot)

- **Cost**: Very high TCO; platform + seats + implementation; mid-market pays for Forecast/Engage they don't use (~$250/user/mo)
- **Smart Trackers**: Legacy keyword tech; 50–100 training examples, 40+ hrs/mo maintenance; sentence embeddings lose context
- **Implementation**: Complex, 3–6 months; API "wonky," custom code for basic export
- **Integration issues**: MS Teams calendar integration sometimes fails, calls unrecorded
- **Support**: Slow response times
- **Usability**: Some say "almost unusable" despite strong AI
- **Scope**: Best for 50+ user teams; overkill for small teams; English-focused

**Sources:** [Peerspot](https://www.peerspot.com/questions/what-needs-improvement-with-gong), [Oliv.ai Gong reviews](https://www.oliv.ai/blog/gong-reviews), [Gong Smart Trackers limitations](https://www.oliv.ai/blog/what-are-gong-smart-trackers)
