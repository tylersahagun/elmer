# Remotion Use Cases and Anti‑Use Cases

This file frames where Remotion adds leverage for AskElephant and where it does not.

## Strong use cases

- **Release highlight videos**: Tie a shipped feature to a clear outcome and CTA.
- **Prototype walkthroughs**: 30–60s recaps after `/proto` to drive alignment.
- **Customer story snippets**: Quote + metric + result, anchored to a persona.
- **Enablement clips**: Short training on new workflows for Sales/CS.
- **Board/leadership summaries**: Visual status updates with evidence and decisions.

## “Proceed with caution” use cases

- **Competitive comparisons**: Only when evidence is verifiable and legal approves.
- **AI‑generated user stories**: Must be grounded in real quotes or data.

## Anti‑use cases (avoid)

- **Generic AI summaries** with no outcome chain or CTA.
- **Automation without trust** (no evidence, no transparency).
- **Long, unfocused videos** (over 2 minutes without a strong reason).
- **Speculative metrics** or unverifiable claims.

## Alignment checks

Before creating a video, confirm:

- Outcome chain exists and is explicit.
- Persona is named and the story is for them.
- Evidence exists (quote, data, or credible release notes).
- The video reinforces trust and transparency.
